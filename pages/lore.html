The Legend of Black Hollow Asylum

Decades ago, Black Hollow Asylum was shut down after a series of disappearances. The official report blamed faulty infrastructure... but locals whisper of strange rituals and inhuman experiments.

Some say the spirits of the patients still roam the halls, seeking justice. Others claim the doctors went mad... or were possessed.

Known Events:

  • ⚠️ 1974: Sudden closure after "The Screaming Night"
  • 👁️ 1980: Sightings of shadowy figures in nearby woods
  • 📻 1992: Radio interference with eerie whispers near the site
